Tomato Dragon

A playful pomodoro timer with task tracking, session controls, and richer progress analytics.

Download GitHub

Highlights

Timer + Sessions

Pomodoro phases with session numbering, start/pause/reset/skip flow, and manual "Start New Session" controls.

Tomato Settings

Daily tomato goals and reset policies: Erry Day, Erry Sess, or Never.

Stats That Matter

This-week and sessions graph modes, work/break include toggles, skipped partial rounds, and recent-rounds timeline.

Data Controls

Backup/load/reset with confirmation plus rich report export for deeper analytics beyond the in-app cards.
